About Us

At Health Plus Associates, we are passionately committed to enhancing community health through top-tier medical services and cutting-edge treatment options. Our mission is to improve the quality of life for our patients while setting the standard for comprehensive and compassionate care.

Established in 1993, our facility has been a cornerstone in the community for over two decades. We offer a wide range of services including general practice, specialized consultations, emergency care, and wellness programs. Our team consists of highly qualified professionals who are dedicated to upholding the highest medical standards.

Our core values are centered around integrity, innovation, and inclusivity. We believe in treating each patient with respect and dignity, embracing technological advancements to enhance treatment outcomes, and providing access to quality healthcare for all members of our community.

We are proud to have impacted thousands of lives through our dedicated service and look forward to continuing our work. We invite you to join us on our journey to better health.
