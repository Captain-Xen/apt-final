Our Story

Health Plus Associates was founded in October 1993 by Drs Orville Nembhard and Tomlin Paul - both graduates of the University of the West Indies Medical Class of 1983. Both Dr Nembhard and Dr. Paul pursued further studies in public health and Dr. Nembhard also holds an MBA from the University of Leicester UK, and a Global Programme in Occupational Health Practice (GPOHP) Certificate from the University of Illinois at Chicago.
The centre, originally located in the Red Hills Mall, began with a complement of three family physicians working from 8:30am to 7:00pm. This was to ensure that there was at least one physician on duty at any one time. The team also had an obstetrician/gynaecologist, dermatologist, dietitian and physiotherapist, and offered laboratory services through Central Medical Labs.

Over the years our clientele and reputation grew and with the increased demand for a wider range of services, we relocated in October 2002 from the Red Hills Road to the newly constructed Boulevard Supercentre.
